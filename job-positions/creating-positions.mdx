## Position Setup

### Essential Fields

- **Title:** Clear, descriptive job title (e.g., "Lead Developer", "Community Moderator")
- **Short Description:** Brief overview for the job board (1-2 sentences)
- **Full Description:** Detailed requirements, responsibilities, and expectations
- **Tags:** Searchable keywords to categorize the position

<Warning>
  Make sure your job descriptions are clear and specific. This helps attract qualified candidates and reduces irrelevant applications.
</Warning>
