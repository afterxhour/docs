## Question Types

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Short Answer" icon="text">
    **Single-line text input for brief responses**

    Best for: Names, usernames, contact info
  </Card>
  <Card title="Long Answer" icon="align-left">
    **Multi-line text area for detailed responses**

    Best for: Experience descriptions, motivations
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multiple Choice" icon="list">
    **Predefined options for standardized answers**

    Best for: Experience levels, preferences
  </Card>
  <Card title="Required Fields" icon="asterisk">
    **Mark questions as mandatory**

    Use sparingly to avoid unanswered questions
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
