## What Makes Crewform Special?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Custom Application Forms">
    Create tailored application forms with multiple question types including text, multiple choice, checkboxes, and file uploads. Build forms that match your recruitment needs perfectly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Public Job Boards">
    Share a beautifully designed public portal where candidates can view open positions and apply directly. Your job board is automatically generated and always up-to-date.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Real-Time Discord Webhooks">
    Receive instant notifications in your Discord server when applications are submitted, reviewed, accepted, or declined. Keep your team in the loop without constant checking.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Team Collaboration">
    Invite collaborators with customizable permissions. Multiple team members can review applications, leave comments, and make decisions together.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Application Management">
    Track applicants through their journey with status management, notes, and decision tracking. Never lose track of a candidate again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Security & Rate Limiting">
    Built-in spam protection, rate limiting, and Roblox account verification ensure quality applications and protect your workspace from abuse.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
