## Step 1: Account Creation

Start by creating your Crewform account and linking your Roblox profile for verification.

<Tip>
  Make sure you're logged into the correct Roblox account before starting the verification process.
</Tip>

## Step 2: Workspace Creation

Create a workspace that represents your Roblox group. You'll need your group ID for this step.

### How to find your Roblox Group ID:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to your group">
    Go to your group page on Roblox
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the URL">
    Look at the URL (e.g., roblox.com/groups/123456/GroupName)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the ID">
    The number after /groups/ is your group ID
  </Step>
</Steps>
