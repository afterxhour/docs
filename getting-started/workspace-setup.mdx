## Workspace Settings

### Basic Information

- **Workspace Name:** The display name shown throughout the platform
- **Description:** Brief overview of your group or organization
- **Contact Information:** How applicants can reach you

### Roblox Integration

- **Group ID:** Links your workspace to your Roblox group
- **Rank Requirements:** Set minimum ranks for applications
- **Group Verification:** Ensure applicants are in your group

<Warning>
  Ensure your Roblox Group ID is correct. An incorrect ID will prevent proper integration.
</Warning>
