<Info>
  Crewform is a professional application management platform designed specifically for Roblox group owners and managers.
</Info>

## What is Crewform?

Crewform provides everything you need to streamline your recruitment process and manage candidates effectively.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Key Features" icon="bolt">
    - Custom application forms with multiple question types
    - Public job boards for your Roblox group
    - Real-time Discord webhook notifications
    - Team collaboration and role management
    - Application tracking and status management
    - Rate limiting and spam protection
  </Card>
  <Card title="Quick Start" icon="rocket">
    1. Sign up and verify your Roblox account
    2. Create a workspace for your group
    3. Set up your first job position
    4. Configure Discord webhooks (optional)
    5. Share your public job board
    6. Start receiving and managing applications
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
